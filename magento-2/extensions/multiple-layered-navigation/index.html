Extension

Multiple Layered Navigation

Magento 2

Every successful online store can give you a possibility to pick more than one characteristic of a product when performing a search. You may also have an opportunity to search your requirements in different categories of an online store. This extension provides the possibility to filter products using multiple choices of their attributes and options. Your clients don't have to scroll through thousands of products due to the limitation of filtering by only one attributes. Your custumers can find their favorite products much faster and easier.
