Extension

News

Magento 2

Your customers want you to be in touch with them and keep them informed about all the exciting news and announcements for your store. The extension has useful tools for you and your managers, which provides the possibility to publish all store news. You can use the widget with the latest news on the home page or other CMS page that will help you to improve customer`s loyalty.

After installing the extension news will appear at the following URL: "www.yourdomain.com/news"
