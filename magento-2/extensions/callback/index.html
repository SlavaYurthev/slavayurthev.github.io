Extension

Callback

Magento 2

Do your customers call you? What will happen if some customers can't call you? Will you lose your sales? This can`t be allowed! Provide your customers with the possibility to request a call back on your website, and you will not lose your customers anymore. The Callback extension makes it possible for the customers to request a call back by phone if they want to know some details about your products or services.

After installing the extension you will see the button on the interface. Customers can click it, fill in the form, and will let you know about their desire to speak with you.
